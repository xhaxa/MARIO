Welcome to Get The Picture!


Are you ready to help world-renowned artists finish their masterpieces in time?

You need to collect sections of the paintings that are scattered all over the museum galleries and take the artist to the blank canvas so that they can put them together.

But careful! You must also avoid the heaps of tourists and getting caught by thieves on the way.
